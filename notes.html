Time Series Forecasting

In the real world, time series forecasting is not univariate as seen in most examples around the web. In general, multiple variables are used which makes the task of a Data Scientist harder but interesting at the same time. To extract meaningful information and statistics from the time series data, there are several time series forecasting methods that comprise the time series analysis. A time-series data which depends on a single variable is known as the Univariate Time Series model. However, when multiple variables are involved to predict the outcome over time, it is referred to as multivariate time series which is the topic of discussion in this blog. In the multivariate time series model, the target variable is not only dependent on multiple features but those are also dependent on each other.
